Detailed Description

Delta formulation that accounts for the orientation of the vorticity vector and a flow-sensitised function to detect 2D flow regions. Delta value is strongly reduced in these regions to accelerate the transition from RANS to LES in hybrid RANS/LES simulations.

Reference: 

    Shur, M. L., Spalart, P. R., Strelets, M. K., & Travin, A. K. (2015).
    An enhanced version of DES with rapid transition
    from RANS to LES in separated flows.
    Flow, turbulence and combustion, 95(4), 709-737.
    DOI:10.1007/s10494-015-9618-0
