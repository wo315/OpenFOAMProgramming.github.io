29Description
30 sixDoFRigidBodyMotionRestraints model. Linear Spring-Damper system.
31
32 Spring-damper system for restraint. Acts as a "soft" rope when the
33 distance between anchor and refAttachmentPt exceed the restLength,
34 a system of spring-damper is activated to simulate a soft rope
35 de-acceleration
