27Class
28 Foam::parFvFieldDistributor
29
30Description
31 Finite volume reconstructor for volume and surface fields.
32
33 Runs in parallel.
34 Reconstructs/redistributes from procMesh to baseMesh.
35 baseMesh is non-zero cells on processor0 only.
